Fight Hunger in LA County

Many people in our community are still recovering from the recent pandemic and the related economic fallout, including the rising prices of everyday necessities.

Your gift today is vital to distributing healthy food to your neighbors in Los Angeles County. We are highly efficient, and 96% of revenues go directly to our programs providing food for children, hardworking families, struggling seniors and more.
